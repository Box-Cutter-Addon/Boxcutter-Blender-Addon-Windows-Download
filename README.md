## 🧠 Overview

**Boxcutter Blender Add-on** is a specialized toolset designed to speed up and simplify hard-surface modeling inside Blender. It is built for artists, designers, and 3D creators who need fast, accurate, and flexible ways to build complex geometric shapes and detailed assets.

The add-on enhances Blender’s workflow by introducing efficient boolean-based modeling, interactive cutting tools, and highly responsive viewport controls. This allows users to quickly construct mechanical forms, refine structures, and iterate designs without slowing down their creative process.

It fits naturally into modern production pipelines, supporting use cases such as game asset creation, concept design, product visualization, architectural modeling, and motion graphics. The toolset is optimized to reduce repetitive actions and keep the focus on creative work rather than technical complexity.

With Boxcutter, users can rapidly prototype ideas, develop detailed hard-surface models, and maintain full control over every stage of the modeling workflow. Its lightweight and intuitive design makes it suitable for both professional studios and individual creators.
